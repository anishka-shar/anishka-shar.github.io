Quick and easy weeknight meal

Prep time: 10 minutes

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillet
  • 1 cup of white rice
  • pak choi
  • 50 ml soy sauce
  • 20 ml fish sauce
  • 10 ml oyster sauce
  • 10 ml honey

Method

Wash rice until water appears clear and put in rice cooker for 20 minutes.

Mix soy sauce, fish sauce, oyster sauce and honey into a bowl to create the marinade.

Cover salmon fillets with marinade and put in oven at 180 degrees for 20 minute.

When the rice and salmon are almost finished, boil pak choi for 3 minutes.

Serve and enjoy!

Delious sweet treat for the whole family

Prep time: 20 minutes

Cooking time: 40 minutes

This showstopping yet simple tiramisu cant be missed!

"This recipe is my go to when hosting friends and family, hopefully it makes its way into oyur kitchen too!" - Chef B, 2024

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons coffee granules
  • 2 tablespoons coffee liqueur
  • 200 ml mascarpone
  • 50 ml condensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 8 sponge fingers
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder

Mehtod

Mix coffee granules with 2 tablespoons of boiling water in a large glass and sitr until fully combined, then add coffee liqueur.

Beat mascarpone, condensed milk and vanilla extract with electric whisk until smooth.

Dip sponge fingers in coffee mixture then lay in serving bowl.

Layer cream mixture on top and sprinkle cocoa powder over the top to finish.

Leave in fridge for 30 minutes then dig in with your loved ones!
